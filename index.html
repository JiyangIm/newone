Top Luxury Hotel in Haeundae, Busan

A luxurious landmark address with sprawling views of the ocean and the famed Gwangan Bridge,
Park Hyatt Busan is situated in the heart of Haeundae, the city’s premier shopping and beach destination,
alongside world-class shopping malls and Busan Exhibition and Convention Center (BEXCO).
Be it for business or leisure travel,
Park Hyatt Busan offers residential comfort with personalized experiences,
welcoming guests with fine art, unique food and beverage offerings, and gracious service.

Rooms + Suites

Park Hyatt Busan offers 269 guestrooms, including 69 suites.
On stepping into a guestroom decorated with beige-toned wood,
visitors can appreciate the inspiring view of the ocean through floor-to-ceiling windows.
Spacious bathrooms with rain showers and bathtubs with custom Le Labo® products further enhance the comfort of guests.
Enjoy our elegant suites featuring additional space, such as separate dining and sitting areas.
Amenities are located throughout the guestroom, allowing you to experience true comforts during your time at Park Hyatt Busan.
